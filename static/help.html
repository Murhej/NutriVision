üéØ Top-1 Accuracy

Definition: The percentage of time the model's #1 prediction (highest confidence) is exactly correct.

Example: Pizza Image

Scenario 1 (Correct):
True Label: pizza
Model's #1 prediction: pizza ‚úÖ
This counts toward Top-1 accuracy!
Scenario 2 (Incorrect):
True Label: pizza
Model's #1 prediction: flatbread
Model's #2 prediction: pizza
This does NOT count toward Top-1 accuracy (even though pizza is in top 3)

Your model's Top-1: 71.73% means the #1 prediction is correct about 7 out of 10 times.

üèÜ Top-3 Accuracy

Definition: The percentage of time the correct answer appears anywhere in the top 3 predictions.

Example: Sushi Image

Scenario 1 (Correct):
True Label: sushi
Model's predictions: 1. sushi, 2. sashimi, 3. tuna_roll ‚úÖ
Counts! (Correct answer in position #1)
Scenario 2 (Also Correct):
True Label: sushi
Model's predictions: 1. sashimi, 2. sushi, 3. tuna_roll ‚úÖ
Still counts! (Correct answer in position #2)
Scenario 3 (Still Correct):
True Label: sushi
Model's predictions: 1. sashimi, 2. tuna_roll, 3. sushi ‚úÖ
Still counts! (Correct answer in position #3)
Scenario 4 (Incorrect):
True Label: sushi
Model's predictions: 1. sashimi, 2. tuna_roll, 3. salmon
Does NOT count (correct answer not in top 3)

Your model's Top-3: 86.33% means the correct answer is in the top 3 about 9 out of 10 times.

üí° Why Top-3 Matters

For your "Smart Meal Logger" use case with human-in-the-loop confirmation:

  • Users see 3 options, not just 1
  • If the right answer is in top 3, users can quickly select it
  • 86% top-3 accuracy = users find the right answer most of the time
  • Much better user experience than forcing a single prediction!

üìä Per-Class Accuracy

In the "Per-Class Performance" section, the accuracy shown is Top-1 accuracy for that specific food.

Example: If "pizza" shows 90% accuracy, it means:

  • When the model sees a pizza image
  • It predicts "pizza" as #1
  • 90% of the time

üéì Summary

  • Top-1 (71.73%): #1 guess is right 71.73% of the time
  • Top-3 (86.33%): Right answer in top 3 guesses 86.33% of the time
  • Per-Class: Shows which specific foods the model knows best

For your app: Top-3 is more important because users pick from 3 options!
